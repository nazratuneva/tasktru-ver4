Personal Profile

As a resident of a community, you can set up your account in minutes and start engaging from your portfolio. Here’s how:

1. Create or Login to your tasktru account.

To view or manage your engagements, you’ll need to be logged in to your tasktru account. Don’t have one? You can create a personal account here for free.

2. Go to your portfolio, click Options, and add your community

Select Create/Add to edit your portfolio, then add your community to begin engaging through tasktru. Please note that in order for you to connect and become an active member of the platform, your community admin will need to pre-register you for approved use.

3. You’re all set up!

Now that you’re up and running, start your engagement actions or manage your existing tasks, responsibilities, or requests.

And you can even share the good news with everyone immediately. The platform will send an alert to friends and other community members to facilitate fast personal connections and meaningful engagements.

Business Profile

As a supplier to a community, you can set up your account in minutes and start engaging from your dashboard. Here’s how:

1. Create or Login to your tasktru account.

To process engagement requests for your community, you’ll need to be logged in to your tasktru account. Don’t have one? You can create a business account here for free, specifically for this purpose. Only staff members/suppliers of communities using the Business-Plus account can use this account to process requests.

2. Add your organization name to become active.

The community admin will need to have pre-registered you in order for you to connect and become an active member of the platform.

3. Go to your Dashboard for actions.

See all requests that have been automatically assigned to you and start processing them instantly. It’s very easy and interactive to process assigned requests with our intuitive dashboard interface.

Business-Plus Profile

As a community Administrator / Leader, you can set up your account in minutes to start managing your community operational demands and safety measures. Here’s how:

1. Create or Login to your tasktru account.

As a community admin, you’ll need to be logged in to your tasktru account to manage community responsibilities, resident interactions, and supplier engagements. Don’t have one? You can create a Business-Plus account here.

2. Subscribe to your desired product from the Options menu.

Subscribe to your desired product type and set up your community functions.

3. You’re all set up!

Now that you’re up and running, start getting insights for community enhancement and other actions to make your community a safe and welcoming place.

The fully integrated engagement platform for managing business operations and lifestyle needs.
